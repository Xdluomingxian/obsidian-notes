### 15-35 分钟：扶贫背景学习

**核心词汇：**

| 概念 | 英文 | 解释 |
|------|------|------|
| **脱贫攻坚** | Poverty Alleviation | Efforts to lift people out of poverty |
| **精准扶贫** | Targeted Poverty Alleviation | Precise, targeted approach |
| **建档立卡** | Register and File | Register poor households |
| **易地搬迁** | Relocation | Move people to better locations |
| **产业扶贫** | Industrial Poverty Alleviation | Develop local industries |
| **教育扶贫** | Educational Poverty Alleviation | Improve education in poor areas |
| **全面小康** | Moderately Prosperous Society | Overall prosperity |

**关键数据：**

```
- 832 poor counties 国家级贫困县
- 1.2 billion yuan invested 投入资金
- 98 million people lifted out of poverty 脱贫人口
- 2020: Goal achieved 目标实现
```

**对比手法句型：**

```
【时间对比】
- In the past, ... Now, ...
- Before ..., ... After ..., ...
- ...used to..., but now...

【条件对比】
- While some..., others...
- Unlike ..., ...
- ...on the other hand...

【程度对比】
- ...much more than before
- ...significantly better than...
- ...far beyond expectations
```

### 35-55 分钟：扶贫故事学习

**观看视频：扶贫干部故事（5 分钟）**

**小组讨论（15 分钟）：**
```
讨论要点：
1. 故事中的主人公是谁？
2. 他/她做了什么？
3. 带来了什么改变？
4. 哪个细节最打动你？
5. 如何用对比突出变化？

每组准备 2 分钟英文汇报
```

**人物故事框架：**

```
【主人公】
- Who is he/she?
- What was his/her role?

【挑战】
- What problems did they face?
- How difficult was the situation?

【行动】
- What did he/she do?
- What sacrifices were made?

【改变】
- What changes happened?
- How are people's lives better?

【意义】
- What does this story tell us?
- Why does it matter?
```

---

### 55-80 分钟：感动型演讲

**演讲主题：** `A Story That Moved Me`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Whose story
- Why it moved you

BODY (3 分钟):
- Background: The situation before
- Challenge: What problems existed
- Action: What was done
- Change: The results (use contrast)

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- What you learned
- How it inspired you
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 20 分钟（每组 1 代表）

**演讲要求：**
- 必须使用对比手法
- 至少包含 3 个具体细节
- 有真实情感表达
