### 15-35 分钟：环保词汇学习

**核心环保词汇：**

| 概念 | 英文 | 解释 |
|------|------|------|
| **生态文明** | Ecological Civilization | Harmony between humans and nature |
| **绿水青山** | Green Mountains and Clear Waters | Environmental protection |
| **碳中和** | Carbon Neutrality | Net-zero carbon emissions |
| **可再生能源** | Renewable Energy | Solar, wind, hydro power |
| **垃圾分类** | Waste Sorting | Garbage classification |
| **生物多样性** | Biodiversity | Variety of life forms |
| **可持续发展** | Sustainable Development | Development without harming environment |

**因果论证句型：**

```
【表达原因】
- The main cause of ... is ...
- This is due to ...
- ...results from...
- Because of ..., ...

【表达结果】
- As a result, ...
- Consequently, ...
- This leads to ...
- The effect is ...

【表达解决方案】
- To address this, we should ...
- One solution is to ...
- We can solve this by ...
- It is essential to ...
```

**因果论证框架：**

```
Problem → Causes → Effects → Solutions

示例：
Problem: Air pollution in cities
Causes: Cars, factories, construction
Effects: Health problems, climate change
Solutions: Public transport, renewable energy, green buildings
```

### 35-55 分钟：环保案例学习

**观看视频：塞罕坝造林故事（5 分钟）**

**小组讨论（15 分钟）：**
```
讨论要点：
1. 塞罕坝以前是什么样子？
2. 为什么会变成荒漠？
3. 人们做了什么？
4. 现在有什么改变？
5. 这个案例告诉我们什么？

每组准备 2 分钟英文汇报
```

**案例分析框架：**

```
【问题】
- What was the environmental problem?
- How serious was it?

【原因】
- What caused this problem?
- Human activities? Natural factors?

【行动】
- What measures were taken?
- Who was involved?

【结果】
- What changes happened?
- Data and evidence

【启示】
- What can we learn?
- How to apply elsewhere?
```

### 55-80 分钟：说服型演讲

**演讲主题：** `Why We Need Green Life`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Hook: A shocking fact or question
- Topic: Why green life matters

BODY (3 分钟):
- Problem: Current environmental issues
- Causes: Why these problems exist
- Effects: Impact on our lives
- Solutions: What we can do

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- Call to action: Specific steps
- Vision: A better future
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 20 分钟（每组 1 代表）

**演讲要求：**
- 必须使用因果论证
- 提出至少 3 个具体行动建议
- 有感染力的呼吁
