### 15-35 分钟：美食词汇学习

**核心美食词汇：**

| 类别 | 词汇 | 英文 |
|------|------|------|
| **主食** | 饺子、包子、面条、米饭 | dumpling, baozi, noodle, rice |
| **菜系** | 川菜、粤菜、鲁菜、淮扬菜 | Sichuan, Cantonese, Lu, Huaiyang cuisine |
| **口味** | 酸、甜、苦、辣、咸 | sour, sweet, bitter, spicy, salty |
| **烹饪** | 炒、蒸、煮、炸、烤 | stir-fry, steam, boil, deep-fry, roast |
| **餐具** | 筷子、碗、盘子、勺子 | chopsticks, bowl, plate, spoon |

**感官描写句型：**

```
【视觉】
- It looks... (golden, crispy, colorful)
- The color is...
- It is shaped like...

【嗅觉】
- It smells... (fragrant, aromatic, delicious)
- The aroma of... fills the air
- I can smell the...

【味觉】
- It tastes... (delicious, savory, tender)
- The flavor is...
- It has a ... taste

【触觉】
- It feels... (crispy, soft, chewy)
- The texture is...
- It melts in your mouth

【听觉】
- It sounds... (sizzling, crunchy)
- You can hear the...
```

### 35-55 分钟：过程描述技巧

**烹饪步骤描述：**

```
【连接词】
First, → Then, → Next, → After that, → Finally,

【常用动词】
- wash, cut, chop, slice, dice
- mix, stir, blend, knead
- heat, cook, fry, steam, boil
- add, put, pour, sprinkle
- serve, decorate, garnish

【示例：包饺子】
First, prepare the filling by mixing meat and vegetables.
Then, cut the dough into small pieces.
Next, roll each piece into a thin wrapper.
After that, put the filling in the center and fold it.
Finally, boil the dumplings in hot water for 10 minutes.
```

**课堂活动：美食演示（15 分钟）**

```
分组任务（4 人一组）：

第 1 组：How to Make Dumplings
第 2 组：How to Make Tea
第 3 组：How to Make Fried Rice
第 4 组：How to Make Hot Pot

要求：
1. 准备 5-6 个步骤
2. 使用连接词
3. 加入感官描写
4. 每组 3 分钟演示

可以带实物或图片辅助
```

### 55-80 分钟：美食演讲

**演讲主题：** `My Favorite Chinese Dish`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Name of the dish
- Why it's your favorite

BODY (2 分钟):
- Appearance: What does it look like?
- Taste: How does it taste?
- Meaning: What does it symbolize?
- Memory: Your personal story

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- Why it represents Chinese culture
- Invitation to try it
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 15 分钟（每组 1 代表）

---

### 80-90 分钟：总结 + 作业布置
