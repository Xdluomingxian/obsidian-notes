### 15-35 分钟：哲学名句学习

**核心哲学概念：**

| 概念 | 英文 | 解释 |
|------|------|------|
| **仁** | Benevolence/Ren | Love, compassion, humaneness |
| **义** | Righteousness/Yi | Justice, moral disposition |
| **礼** | Propriety/Li | Ritual, proper conduct |
| **智** | Wisdom/Zhi | Knowledge, understanding |
| **信** | Integrity/Xin | Trustworthiness, honesty |
| **道** | Tao/Dao | The Way, natural order |
| **德** | Virtue/De | Moral power, virtue |

**孔子名句（Confucius Quotes）：**

```
1. "己所不欲，勿施于人。"
   "Do not do to others what you do not want done to yourself."

2. "学而时习之，不亦说乎？"
   "Is it not pleasant to learn and to practice what you have learned?"

3. "三人行，必有我师焉。"
   "When three walk together, there must be one who can be my teacher."

4. "知之者不如好之者，好之者不如乐之者。"
   "To know it is not as good as to love it; to love it is not as good as to delight in it."
```

**老子名句（Laozi Quotes）：**

```
1. "道可道，非常道。"
   "The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao."

2. "千里之行，始于足下。"
   "A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step."

3. "知人者智，自知者明。"
   "Knowing others is wisdom; knowing yourself is enlightenment."

4. "上善若水。"
   "The highest goodness is like water."
```

**引用经典技巧：**

```
【引入引用】
- As Confucius said, "..."
- In the words of Laozi, "..."
- There is an old Chinese saying, "..."

【解释引用】
- This means that...
- What it tells us is...
- The wisdom behind this is...

【联系现实】
- This is still relevant today because...
- In modern life, we can see...
- This teaches us to...
```

### 35-55 分钟：东西方哲学对比

**对比要点：**

| 维度 | 中国哲学 | 西方哲学 |
|------|----------|----------|
| **关注点** | 人生、伦理、处世 | 存在、知识、逻辑 |
| **方法** | 直觉、体悟 | 理性、论证 |
| **表达** | 格言、对话 | 论文、体系 |
| **代表** | 孔子、老子 | 苏格拉底、柏拉图 |

**课堂活动：哲学辩论（20 分钟）**

```
辩论主题：Confucius vs. Socrates - Who is More Relevant Today?

正方 (Pro-Confucius):
- 儒家思想对现代社会的价值
- 仁爱、礼仪的现实意义
- 家庭观念的重要性

反方 (Pro-Socrates):
- 苏格拉底方法的现代应用
- 批判性思维的重要性
- 个人理性的价值

辩论流程：
1. 正方立论（3 分钟）
2. 反方立论（3 分钟）
3. 自由辩论（5 分钟）
4. 总结陈词（各 2 分钟）

要求：
- 至少引用 2 句经典名句
- 使用对比句型
- 联系现实生活
```

### 55-80 分钟：期中演讲准备

**期中演讲主题：** `A Chinese Philosophy That Inspires Me`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Which philosophy/philosopher
- Why it inspires you

BODY (4 分钟):
- Core idea: What is the main teaching?
- Quote: Share one famous saying
- Application: How do you apply it?
- Impact: What change has it brought?

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- Why it matters today
- Your commitment
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 15 分钟（部分学生展示）
**正式演讲：** 下周课堂进行
