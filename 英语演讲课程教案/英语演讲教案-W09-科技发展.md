### 15-35 分钟：科技词汇学习

**核心科技词汇：**

| 领域 | 词汇 | 英文 |
|------|------|------|
| **通信** | 5G、基站、信号 | 5G, base station, signal |
| **交通** | 高铁、地铁、自动驾驶 | high-speed rail, subway, autonomous driving |
| **航天** | 卫星、火箭、空间站 | satellite, rocket, space station |
| **支付** | 移动支付、二维码、扫码 | mobile payment, QR code, scan |
| **AI** | 人工智能、算法、大数据 | AI, algorithm, big data |

**数据引用句型：**

```
【引入数据】
- According to..., ...
- Statistics show that...
- Research indicates that...
- Data from ... suggests that...

【描述趋势】
- ...has increased/decreased by...
- There is a growing trend of...
- ...is on the rise/decline
- The number has doubled/tripled

【表达预测】
- It is expected that...
- Experts predict that...
- ...is likely to...
- The trend will continue...
```

**数据引用示例：**

```
✅ 正确示例：
"According to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, 
China has built over 2.3 million 5G base stations by 2025, 
accounting for 60% of the global total."

❌ 错误示例：
"China has many 5G base stations." (太模糊，无数据支撑)
```

### 35-55 分钟：趋势描述技巧

**趋势描述框架：**

```
【过去】
- In the past decade, ...
- Ten years ago, ...
- Before ..., people used to...

【现在】
- Today, ...
- Currently, ...
- At present, ...

【未来】
- In the next five years, ...
- By 2030, ...
- It is expected that...
```

**课堂活动：科技成就展示（15 分钟）**

```
分组任务（4 人一组）：

第 1 组：5G Technology in China
第 2 组：High-speed Rail Network
第 3 组：Space Exploration (Chang'e, Tiangong)
第 4 组：Mobile Payment Revolution

要求：
1. 收集 3-5 个关键数据
2. 描述发展趋势
3. 分析影响和意义
4. 每组 3 分钟展示

可以使用图表辅助
```

### 55-80 分钟：信息型演讲

**演讲主题：** `China's Tech Achievement That Impresses Me`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Which achievement
- Why it impresses you

BODY (3 分钟):
- Background: When did it start?
- Data: Key statistics (3-5 个)
- Comparison: vs. other countries
- Impact: How it changed lives

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- What it shows about China
- Your pride/reflection
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 20 分钟（每组 1 代表）
