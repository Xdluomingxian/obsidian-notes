### 15-35 分钟：艺术词汇学习

**核心艺术词汇：**

| 艺术形式 | 英文 | 核心元素 |
|----------|------|----------|
| **书法** | Calligraphy | 笔 brush, 墨 ink, 纸 paper, 砚 inkstone |
| **国画** | Chinese Painting | 山水 landscape, 花鸟 flower-bird, 人物 figure |
| **京剧** | Peking Opera | 脸谱 facial makeup, 唱腔 singing, 服饰 costume |
| **剪纸** | Paper Cutting | 图案 pattern, 红色 red, 对称 symmetry |
| **陶瓷** | Porcelain | 青花 blue and white, 釉 glaze, 窑 kiln |

**比喻修辞句型：**

```
【明喻 Simile】
- ...is like...
- ...as ... as...
- It looks as if...

示例：
"The brush strokes are like dancing dragons."
"The ink is as black as night."

【暗喻 Metaphor】
- ...is...
- The ... of...

示例：
"Each stroke is a poem."
"The painting is a window to nature."

【拟人 Personification】
- The ... seems to...
- It appears to...

示例：
"The mountains seem to whisper ancient secrets."
"The flowers appear to smile at viewers."
```

---

### 35-55 分钟：艺术鉴赏表达

**描述艺术作品的框架：**

```
【整体印象】
- At first glance, it looks...
- The overall feeling is...
- It gives me a sense of...

【细节描写】
- In the foreground/background...
- The use of color/light/shadow...
- The brush strokes are...

【情感反应】
- It makes me feel...
- I am impressed by...
- It reminds me of...

【文化解读】
- It symbolizes...
- It represents...
- The meaning behind it is...
```

**课堂活动：艺术鉴赏（15 分钟）**

```
分组任务（4 人一组）：

第 1 组：欣赏一幅山水画
第 2 组：欣赏一幅书法作品
第 3 组：欣赏一张京剧脸谱
第 4 组：欣赏一幅剪纸作品

讨论要点：
1. 你看到了什么？（视觉）
2. 给你什么感觉？（情感）
3. 有什么象征意义？（文化）
4. 用 1 个比喻来形容它

每组准备 2 分钟英文汇报
```

---

### 55-80 分钟：艺术演讲

**演讲主题：** `The Beauty of a Traditional Art`

**结构框架：**
```
OPENING (30 秒):
- Name of the art form
- Why you chose it

BODY (3 分钟):
- History: When did it start?
- Features: What makes it unique?
- Appreciation: Describe one work
- Meaning: What does it represent?

CONCLUSION (30 秒):
- Why it matters today
- Your personal connection
```

**准备时间：** 10 分钟
**演讲时间：** 20 分钟（每组 1-2 代表）

---
